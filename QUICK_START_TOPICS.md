## üöÄ 5-Minute Guide

### 1. First Launch
When you first open the upgraded app, your existing exercises will be automatically organized into a topic called **"My Exercises"**. Everything works exactly as before!

---

### 2. Create Your First Topic

**Left Sidebar (Topics):**
1. Type: `telc B1 Dative Practice`
2. Description: `Complete course for exam preparation`
3. Click **"Create Topic"**

---

### 3. Add Multiple Exercises at Once

**Middle Sidebar (Exercises):**
1. Click **"Add Multiple Exercises"**
2. Copy this example and paste it:

### 4. Practice as Usual

1. Click an exercise from the middle column
2. Click the **"Practice"** tab
3. Answer questions
4. Track your progress!
